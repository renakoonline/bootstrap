Entry level and Interships

Optimum Solutions offers exciting opportunities to the fresh degree or diploma holders in Information & Communications Technology through the Graduate Trainee program. Through this program, Optimum Solutions provides a platform to expand your professional and academic horizon and help you prepare for a successful career ahead. Other pre-requisites are:

  • Singapore Citizens
  • Fresh graduates from one of the local universities/polytechnics
  • Meet additional selection requirements set by Optimum Solution
On successful completion of the program, the trainees may be offered full time roles with Optimum Solutions.

Please register your interests by sending us your profile details on solutions@theoptimum.net.

Internships
Optimum Solutions also offers internships to Singapore Citizens studying at one of the local universities or polytechnics in ICT or related streams who meet basic selection requirements set by Optimum Solutions.

Please register your interests by sending us your profile details on solutions@theoptimum.net.






We are committed to the principles of Fair Employment Practices to
“Recruit and select employees on the basis of merit (such as skills, experience or ability to perform the job), and regardless of age, race, gender, religion, family status or disability.”
