Custom Solutions

In order to solve their specific business problems or to manage their strategic processes differently,  many organization (both large and small) implement custom solutions. While some custom solutions are focused on improving operational efficiencies, others serve a more purpose of strategically differentiating a company from its competition or offering a highly innovative way of solving a business problem. Some companies have a unique business model that cannot be easily integrated into a packaged software. A custom solution allows them to fully leverage their unique propositions.

Optimum Solutions partners with companies and their IT organizations to help implement their differentiated business processes into a technology solution in a timely and cost effective manner. We, therefore, help empower business with technology.

We have proven experience and expertise in custom application development and maintenance across a range of technologies. We have implemented a range of solutions from simple web sites to mission critical business applications. We have worked on Microsoft, Java as well as Open-source technologies, depending on the requirements of our customers.

Optimum not only helps in Application development, but also in on-going application maintenance and support. We believe, it is critical to keep a small team to help stabilize the application and make on-going improvements to align the application to changing business needs.

Our custom application development and maintenance services are delivered using a blended onsite / offshore model depending on the needs of the customers. We extensively use our offshore based delivery in order to keep the service cost-efficient and to take advantage of better resource availability and scale. The onsite representatives help ensure tight coordination with customer representatives. This overall blend helps us remain well aligned with customer requirements and yet deliver the services in a cost-effective manner.
