Enterprise Applications

With rapid growth of Enterprise Applications (Oracle / SAP) solutions and platforms, companies are focusing on making their Oracle / SAP investments work for them. They need to develop an overall strategy for end to end information delivery and adapt technology effectively in order to achieve their goals.

Optimum helps companies translate their investments in Enterprise applications and platforms into tangible implementations and solutions that result in real returns. While Oracle & SAP have several solutions for different verticals, Optimum helps adapt those solutions to your specific organizational requirements and aligns them with your business strategy.

Our Enterprise Applications practice has grown on the basis of years of service to several customers in Singapore, the US and India. Our practice is focused around the following solution areas:

  • Enterprise Resource Planning
    • Financial Management
  • Supply Chain Management
    • Supply Chain Planning
    • Logistics and Transportation Management
    • Order Management and Pricing
  • Customer Relationship Management
  • Manufacturing
  • Procurement
  • Middleware (Oracle Fusion / SAP NetWeaver)
We offer services not only to help you implement Oracle / SAP products and technologies, but also to sustain your investments. Our range of services include:
  • Functional / Technical Consulting (Requirement Analysis, Design)
  • Implementations (Customization, Configuration, Deployment)
  • Migrations (from previous versions to new ones)
  • Applications Management Services (to sustain applications)
  • Managed Services (to sustain application and infrastructure)
The above services are delivered either onsite or offshore depending on the needs of the customers. We use our offshore based delivery center extensively in our services, in order to keep the service cost-efficient without compromising the overall effectiveness of the engagement.
